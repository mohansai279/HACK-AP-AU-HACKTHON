What is Facial Emotion Recognition?

Facial Emotion Recognition is a feature of NovaMind that analyzes facial expressions to identify emotions. It enhances the understanding of user emotions and provides tailored recommendations based on facial cues.

How does Online Therapy work?

Online Therapy on NovaMind allows users to connect with mental health professionals virtually. Through secure and confidential sessions, individuals can receive therapeutic support and guidance from the comfort of their own space.

What is an AI Psychologist?

An AI Psychologist on NovaMind is a virtual assistant that provides guidance and recommendations. Using advanced algorithms, it understands user queries and offers support on various mental health topics.
